      PRIVACY POLICY

      Evtech Automotive Ltd

      Last updated: June 2026

      1. Introduction

      Evtech Automotive Ltd (“we”, “our”, “us”) operates the website evtech.co.nz and provides automotive repair, diagnostic, and electrical services. We are committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020. By using our website or services, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

      2. Information We Collect

      2.1 Personal Information

      We may collect:

      • Name
      • Phone number
      • Email address
      • Vehicle details (make, model, registration, VIN, issues reported)
      • Booking and service information
      • Messages submitted through our contact form or email

      2.2 Automatically Collected Information

      When you visit our website, we may collect:

      • IP address
      • Browser and device information
      • Pages visited and time spent
      • Cookies and analytics data

      3. How We Use Your Information

      We use your information to:

      • Provide and manage automotive services
      • Respond to enquiries and bookings
      • Send updates, quotes, and service reminders
      • Improve our website and customer experience
      • Maintain internal records
      • Comply with New Zealand legal obligations

      We do not sell or rent your personal information.

      4. Legal Basis for Collecting Information

      Under the Privacy Act 2020, we collect personal information only when:

      • It is necessary for our business operations
      • You provide it voluntarily
      • You consent to its use
      • We are legally required to collect it

      5. Cookies

      Our website uses cookies for:

      • Analytics
      • Performance monitoring
      • Improving user experience

      You may disable cookies in your browser settings.

      6. Sharing Your Information

      We may share your information with:

      • Service providers assisting with bookings, analytics, or website hosting
      • Suppliers or specialists involved in your vehicle repair (only when necessary)
      • Government agencies if required by law

      We do not share your information with third parties for marketing.

      7. Data Storage & Security

      We take reasonable steps to protect your information from:

      • Loss
      • Misuse
      • Unauthorised access
      • Disclosure

      However, no online system is completely secure.

      8. Your Rights (Privacy Act 2020)

      You have the right to:

      • Access your personal information
      • Request correction of inaccurate information
      • Request deletion where appropriate
      • Make a privacy complaint

      To exercise these rights, contact us at: Evtech Automotive Ltd Phone: 0800 900 911 Website: evtech.co.nz/contact-us

      9. Third‑Party Links

      Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

      10. Changes to This Policy

      We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Updates will be posted on this page.
