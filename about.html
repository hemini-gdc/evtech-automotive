    OUR MISSION

    Our Mission Is To Expertly Service Your Vehicle

    Founded on the principles of precision engineering and outstanding customer service, EVtech Automotive has grown to become the premier high-voltage repair specialist network in the country.

    We believe in keeping clean transport reliable. By offering high-quality cell replacement, software configuration, and thermal system maintenance, we help clients avoid costly full pack replacements and reduce electronic waste.

    • EV Engineering Level 5 Certified Technicians
    • 100% compliant high-voltage testing equipment
    • Environmentally friendly lithium battery recycling policy
    • Friendly expert advice and full SOH certifications
    OUR CORE VALUES

    What Guides EVtech Automotive

    Safety First

    High-voltage systems require strict safety standards. We enforce comprehensive safety protocols to protect our team, our customers, and their valuable assets.

    Innovation

    EV technology updates continuously. We invest heavily in software programming suites, battery cell cyclers, and regular technical upskilling.

    Sustainability

    By repairing rather than discarding whole battery packs, we keep battery manufacturing resources cycling longer and minimize carbon footprints.
